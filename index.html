---Tips And Tricks---

Working Your Upper Body & Core

1) Do push-ups to strengthen your triceps, shoulders, and chest.

2) Do handstands against the wall to build your shoulders and back.

3) Do dips to work out your arms.

4) Do planks

5) Do crunches to build your abs and core.

6) Use a gallon of milk, a heavy book, or home dumbbells to perform basic curls.


Working Your Lower Body

1) Use intense bursts of cardio to build leg muscles quickly.

2) Do wall sits.

3) Do squats.

4) Do donkey kicks.

5) Try glute bridges.

6) Work on your lunges.


Forming a Muscle-Building Routine

1) Make a workout schedule that hits every muscle group twice a week.

2) Focus on great form, not extra repetitions, to gain muscle quickly and safely.

3) Do yoga to stretch muscles with a full-body workout.

4) Push yourself so that the last 2-3 reps of every set are difficult, but not impossible.

5) Eat a balanced diet that is heavy on protein but low on fat.

6) Consider buying some basic home-gym equipment if you're serious about working out.
